Organize your life's data

Understand your behaviour by tracking everything together.

Combine data from services you already use, to gain insights on various aspects of your life such as - productivity, activity and finances. You can include a variety of services and devices. Bring your activity from your phone or fitness tracker, and add other services like your calendar, screen time and others for a better picture on what you're up to.

Get started

Panalytics is a platform that allows you to track everything in one place. Panalytics works best when you bring the data you already have by connecting many services from this list.

You can choose the data that you want to bring to the panalytics platform. You have the freedom to select your device and apps to see what we can import.
